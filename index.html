About Me

I love rock climbing and science and making fruit smoothies

What I'm Interested In

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts. Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the Semantics, a large language ocean. A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia.

It is a paradisematic country, in which roasted parts of sentences fly into your mouth. Even the all-powerful Pointing has no control about the blind texts it is an almost unorthographic life One day.

April 2016

nature

"Waking up homeostasis"

Homeostatic mechanisms are thought to maintain overall levels of activity within a cortical network within a narrow range. However, how the 'set point' of firing activity is determined and whether homeostatic mechanisms occur continually or during specific behavioural states such as during wake or sleep is not clear.

March 2016

Cell

Neuronal Firing Rate Homeostasis Is Inhibited by Sleep and Promoted by Wake

Here we use chronic multielectrode recordings to monitor firing rates in visual cortex of freely behaving rats during chronic monocular visual deprivation... and establish that homeostatic mechanisms within the intact CNS act to stabilize neuronal firing rates in the face of sustained sensory perturbations.
